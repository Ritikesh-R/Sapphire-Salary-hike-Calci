What is Salary Hike Calculator?

Salary hike: What is a hike in salary?

A salary hike, also known as an increment, is an increase in the current wage by a fixed percentage of the current salary. It is known as the percentage increase.

How to Calculate New Salary Using Hike Percentage?

  1. Step 1: Convert the hike percentage into decimals.
  2. Step 2: Multiply it by your current salary. Now, this is your increment/hike amount.
  3. Step 3: Add the hike amount to your current salary. The result is your new net salary after the hike.

Salary Hike Formula:

New Net Salary = Hike Percentage (in decimal) * Current Salary + Current Salary

Let us understand the math by calculating a 30% hike in salary.

  1. Step 1: 30% hike is 30/100, which is 0.3.
  2. Step 2: Let us imagine the current salary being ₹20,000. So, 20,000 * 0.3 is ₹6,000.
  3. Step 3: Adding the increment of ₹6,000 to the current salary makes it ₹26,000. Now, this is the new net salary after the hike.

How to Calculate the Salary Hike Percentage?

  1. Step 1: Calculate the difference between your new salary after the hike and your old salary.
  2. Step 2: Divide the result by your old salary.
  3. Step 3: Multiply the obtained number by 100. Now, this is your hike percentage with respect to your old salary.

Salary Hike Percentage Calculation Formula:

Hike Percentage = (New Salary - Old Salary) / Old Salary * 100

Let us understand this with an example.

  1. Let us imagine the new salary to be ₹25,000 and the old salary to be ₹20,000.
  2. Step 1: The difference between the new and old salary is ₹5,000.
  3. Step 2: Now, 5,000 / 20,000 is 0.25.
  4. Step 3: Multiplying 0.25 by 100 gives you 25%. Hence, the salary hike is 25%.
